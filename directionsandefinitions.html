Directions and Definitions

Description

When a video starts, you will press the button of the action that corresponds with what you see on the screen. If you are able to find the action within a second or two of it being done, that is great. If not, move on to the current action. Each video will place 3 times and you can make sure to track the action later.

Each time you press an action button, the action, time you pressed it, your username and url of the video will be recorded.
